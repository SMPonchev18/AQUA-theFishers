We are not alone on this planet

Life at sea in Europe is not in good condition. The poor state of ecosystems shows an immediate impact on many animals and plants that inhabit the aquatic environment and reach other species, as well as people who depend on the availability of clean water. The state of Europe's seas is very difficult, mainly advice on overfishing and climate change, while freshwater bodies suffer from overloading of nutrients and change of location. Chemical pollution has a negative impact on both freshwater and marine ecosystems.
